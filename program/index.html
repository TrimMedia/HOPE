Each year has a different theme or leadership focus. The training includes a one-week course, usually in the summer, along with service projects and leadership opportunities throughout the school year.

Year 1 “Why My Voice Is Important”

Speech Writing, Public Speaking, T-Shirt Design, iMovie Creation on the 7 Habits of Highly Effective Teens, Five Practices of Exemplary Leadership, Service Projects.

Year 2 “How My Passion Impacts the World”

Review of Five Practices of Exemplary Leadership, Writing Reflections, Speech Writing, Public Speaking, Research Non-profits, Team Presentations, iMovie Creation promoting your school, Volunteer hours at a local non-profit.

Year 3 “How My Optimism Leads to a Greater Future”

Career Interest Inventory, Research Career Options, Develop plan/goals for future, Individual Presentation “Roadmap to My Future,” Speech Writing, Public Speaking, College Visits, Job Shadowing.

Beyond Year 3:

Mentor Training, Assist Teachers at Year 1 &2 Programs, Mentor younger Cadets, Seminars on Leadership.
